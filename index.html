    Buran-Pass

    Day 1st

    Via Janglikh Village, we will reach Simbhir place, where tea break and breakfast will be done. After having breakfast we will leave for Diara, where we will have lunch and dinner and we will stay there for the first night.

    Day 2nd

    In the morning after having breakfast in Diara we will leave for the onward journey. It took the whole day to walk, enjoying the beautiful views on the way, we will reach the place of Litham. Where we will stay for lunch, dinner and 2nd night.

    Day 3rd

    After breakfast in the morning we leave for Buren-Pass. From here the journey of Buren Pass starts. On the way via Dunda Place, we will continue on for Buren-Pass.

    End of Track

    After this journey of days and nights, this tracking ends at Buren-Pass.

    Hidden-Side of Chanshal

    Day 1st

    On the first day you will reach Kharshali Village from Shimla through our taxi service, where you will be taken to the first camp site. Dinner and first night stay will be provided to you at the first camp site.

    Day 2nd

    After breakfast we will go to Kharshali Temple. This is a very famous and mythological temple here. After visiting the temple we proceed to our next destination named Pangu-Top, where lunch will be provided. After having lunch we will leave for our next destination which is named Saru-Taal. We will have dinner at Saru-Taal and will also have 2nd night stay. Also there will be some activities after dinner such as music party etc.

    Day 3rd

    After having breakfast in the morning, we will leave for a one of the higher places of Chanshal, whose name is Junafad, where lunch will be provided. After lunch, we will proceed to our 2nd camp site,there will be dinner and 3rd night stay. Like last night, some activities will be done on this night also.

    End of Track

    After having breakfast, the track will start for zero-point place. Taxi will be provided at the zero-point from where you will return to Shimla.

    Chanshal Road Trip

    Trip

    The trip starts from Chanshal Camps and Resort(Mandli) and ends at Jiskoon Village.

    Day 1st

    We will start from Chanshal Camps & Resort(Mandli) and reach Chanshal via Kharsali Village.There will be a small gathering, group photos will be taken and refreshments will be given. After some time we will leave from Chanshal for our second destination Dodra-Kwar.Dinner and first night stay will be held at Dodra-Kwar.

    Day 2nd

    After breakfast in the morning, we will leave for Jiskoon Village. After reaching the village, we will enjoy the beautiful scenery there.After that we will return from Jiskoon to Kharshali via Dodra-Kwar.There will be dinner and 2nd night stay at Kharshali camp site.

    End of the Track

    In the morning after breakfast, we will leave for Chanshal Camps & Resort(Mandli).

    Mountain Expedition Chanshal C-1

    Race

    It is a 29km walk race which will start from Kharsali village and run till Junafad.

    Track

    During the race, you will have to face mud roads, rocky roads, unpaved roads, and snowy roads which will be full of entertainment. Along with this, you will also see a lake during the race.
